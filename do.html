What we do

We follow 5 simple steps - Sign Up, Join Your Group, Brainstorm, Code + Collaborate, Present

Sign Up
Join a Group
Brainstorm
Code
Present

Sign up - You sign up on our contact us page by filling in your details.

Join Your Group - After you were signed up and verified, you will be assigned a group with people from all over the world. You get assigned few timings to call (based of your timezone) and are put in a mutual group.

Brainstorm - What problem do you want to solve? How do you want to solve it? Is this feasible? These are questions you have to answer during your brainstorm period.

Code + Collaborate - Who makes what part? Who works on the front end? Who works on the back end? It is time to code and collaborate with your peers!

Present - Presentations are taken place bi-annually with the presence of a prominent person in the field of robotics and all the other sub-clubs in this international robotics club. Together, as a group, there are reflections taken place on your projects and more ideas on how to improve it.
